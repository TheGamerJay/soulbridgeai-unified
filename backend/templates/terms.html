Anonymous Community Privacy

Our anonymous community system is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • True Anonymity: Community posts are never linked to your username or profile information
  • Hash-Based System: We use one-way cryptographic hashes to enable features like muting while preserving anonymity
  • Content-Only Storage: We store only the content of posts, selected category, and associated companion avatar
  • No Direct Messaging: Users cannot contact each other directly through the community system
  • Viewer-Scoped Muting: Muting preferences are stored locally to your account and don't reveal information about other users
  • Automatic Content Filtering: Posts are automatically scanned for personal information and blocked before publication
  • Limited Metadata: We only store necessary data for moderation and service improvement, not user behavior tracking

What We Don't Store: We do not store IP addresses, device fingerprints, or any other data that could be used to de-anonymize community posts. All identifying information is separated from community content.
